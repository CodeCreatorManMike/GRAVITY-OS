A glanceable desk & bedside object. A single circular e-ink display, ~100mm, faced at a slight upward tilt 40–60cm from the eye. Value is read in under two seconds without a touch — though tap, swipe and long-press are there when you reach for it. Three visual directions, each treating the circle as an instrument: perimeter for status, mid-ring for context, centre for the one thing that matters.

- Then narrow: ask one specific follow-up to get what you still need
- Use embedded examples to guide answers: instead of "are you a morning person?" say "do you do your best work early — like before 10am — or does your brain only really switch on later in the day?"

- For communication say: "when you are off track — does it help more to be told bluntly, shown the data, asked what happened, or just gently reminded?"
